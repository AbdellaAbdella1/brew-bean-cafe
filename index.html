Our Coffee Story

From Bean to Cup

At Brew & Bean Caf√©, our journey begins in the misty highlands of Ethiopia, where coffee was first discovered. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, ensuring every cup supports both quality and ethical practices.

The Art of Roasting

Our master roaster carefully roasts each batch to perfection, bringing out unique flavor profiles. Whether it's the chocolatey notes of Brazilian beans or the fruity undertones of Kenyan AA, we celebrate coffee's diversity.

Our Philosophy

We believe coffee is more than a drink - it's a moment of connection. Each cup should tell a story, from the farmer who grew it to the barista who crafts it with care.

Our Passion for Ethiopian Coffee

We take pride in providing high-quality Ethiopian coffee that allows one to experience the unique Italian lifestyle, which is full of passion, creativity, and attention to detail. In every sip you can feel the Ethiopian joy of life!

Independent, Family Run, Dog Friendly

We are a place for anyone looking for a quiet place to relax, catch up with friends, study, read, work, or simply watch the world go by.
